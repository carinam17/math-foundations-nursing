    Conversion Tables¶

    Quick reference for the most common unit conversions in nursing practice.

    Weight¶

    From To Multiply by
    kg g × 1000
    g mg × 1000
    mg mcg × 1000
    g kg ÷ 1000
    mg g ÷ 1000
    mcg mg ÷ 1000
    lb kg ÷ 2.2
    kg lb × 2.2

    Weight Staircase

    \[\text{kg} \rightarrow \text{g} \rightarrow \text{mg} \rightarrow \text{mcg}\]

    Moving right (larger to smaller unit) — multiply by 1000

    Moving left (smaller to larger unit) — divide by 1000

    Volume¶

    From To Multiply by
    L mL × 1000
    mL L ÷ 1000
    tsp mL × 5
    tbsp mL × 15
    fl oz mL × 30
    cup mL × 240

    Household to Metric¶

    Household Metric
    1 tsp 5 mL
    1 tbsp 15 mL
    1 fl oz 30 mL
    1 cup 240 mL
    1 pint 480 mL
    1 quart 960 mL
    1 oz 30 g
    1 lb 453.6 g
    1 lb 0.454 kg
    1 kg 2.2 lb

    Time¶

    From To Conversion
    hours minutes × 60
    minutes hours ÷ 60

    IV Drop Factors¶

    Tubing Type Drop Factor
    Macrodrip 10 gtt/mL
    Macrodrip 15 gtt/mL
    Macrodrip 20 gtt/mL
    Microdrip 60 gtt/mL

    Microdrip Shortcut

    With microdrip tubing (60 gtt/mL):

    \[\text{gtt/min} = \text{mL/hr}\]

    Common Metric Prefixes¶

    Prefix Symbol Value
    kilo k × 1000
    (base) — × 1
    milli m × 0.001
    micro mc × 0.000001

    Safety Abbreviations

    Write This Never This Reason
    0.5 mg .5 mg Missing leading zero
    5 mg 5.0 mg Trailing zero
    mcg µg Misread as mg
    units U Misread as zero
