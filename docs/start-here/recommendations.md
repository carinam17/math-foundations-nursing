# Recommendations

A few habits make this material easier to learn:

- **Study consistently.** Steady practice promotes better learning outcomes
  than cramming.
- **Favor shorter, frequent sessions** over infrequent marathon ones.
- **If you get stuck, write down what you know.** Putting the knowns on paper
  often reveals the next step on its own.
- **Show your work and check your reasoning** — a habit worth building here,
  and essential in clinical practice.

None of this is about being fast. It is about building fluency you can rely
on when it matters.
