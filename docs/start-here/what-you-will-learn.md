# What You Will Learn

This course is organized into six modules. Each builds on the one before it,
so working through them in order will serve you best.

1. **Arithmetic** — whole numbers, fractions, decimals, rounding, and
   percentages: the number sense every later calculation depends on.
2. **Units of Measurement & Conversions** — the metric system, household
   measurements, and how to convert confidently between units.
3. **Ratios, Proportions & Dimensional Analysis** — setting up and solving
   clinical problems by letting the units guide the arrangement.
4. **Dosage Calculations** — oral and liquid medications, weight-based
   dosing, and pediatric dosing.
5. **IV & Infusion Calculations** — IV medications, flow rates, drip rates,
   and infusion time.
6. **Return & Reinforce** — targeted review and mixed practice to consolidate
   everything and check your understanding before you finish.

Remember, all skills can be practiced and improved. Progress comes from
steady effort, not speed.
