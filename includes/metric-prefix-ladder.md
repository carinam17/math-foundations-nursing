| Prefix | Symbol | Relative to base unit |
|---|---|---|
| **kilo** | k | × 1,000 |
| *(base)* | g, L, m | × 1 |
| **milli** | m | ÷ 1,000 |
| **micro** | mc *(nursing)*, μ | ÷ 1,000,000 |

To convert between metric units: count the steps between prefixes on the ladder, then multiply or divide by the appropriate factor of 1,000.
